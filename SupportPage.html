Magic Keyboard - Star Ripples

Magic Keyboard - Star Ripples is a customizable iOS keyboard designed for relaxation and visual enjoyment. It features soft color palettes, blur effects, and smooth animations.

The main feature of Magic Keyboard - Star Ripples is an interactive color-mixing animation. Touch and move your finger across the keyboard to gently blend colors, simulating the flow of water for a calming experience.

You can choose from pre-designed keyboard styles or create your own by adjusting colors, effects, and layouts. Save your designs and switch between them anytime.

A row of random positive emojis appears above the keyboard, adding a light and playful touch while you type.

Available on iPhone.
