# Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how **Magic Keyboard – Star Ripples** ("we", "our", "us", or the "Application") handles information when you use the Application.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. The Application is designed so that your keyboard input remains private and on your device.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data

We do **not** collect, store, or transmit personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, identifiers, or user accounts.

### 1.2 Keyboard Input

Magic Keyboard – Star Ripples functions as a custom keyboard.

- All text typed using the keyboard remains **entirely on your device**
- Keyboard input is **not collected, logged, stored, analyzed, or transmitted**
- Typed content is **never sent** to the developer or to any third party

The Application does **not** record keystrokes or access typed content for any purpose.

### 1.3 Usage Data

We may collect **limited, non-identifiable usage data**, such as:
- feature usage statistics
- crash reports
- basic performance metrics

This data:
- does **not** include keyboard input
- does **not** identify you personally
- is used solely to improve application stability and performance

---

## 2. Subscription and Payments

All payments and subscription management are handled exclusively by **Apple** through the App Store.

- We do **not** receive or store payment details
- We do **not** have access to your Apple ID or billing information

Subscription data is governed by Apple’s own privacy and security policies.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Since the Application does not collect or store personal data or keyboard input, sensitive user data is not retained on our servers.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to ensure the Application operates securely and as intended.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The Application may use Apple-provided services, such as:
- App Store billing
- App Store analytics
- system-level diagnostics

These services are governed by **Apple’s Privacy Policy**.  
We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or external analytics platforms that collect personal data.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

The Application is **not intended for children under the age of 13**.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data in connection with the Application, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights related to your personal data, including the right to:
- access
- correction
- deletion
- restriction or objection to processing
- data portability

Because Magic Keyboard – Star Ripples does **not** collect or store personal data or keyboard input, most of these rights do not apply in practice.

---

## 7. GDPR (European Union) Disclosure

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you are entitled to rights under the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

### Important Clarification

Magic Keyboard – Star Ripples does **not** collect, store, process, or transmit:
- personal data
- keyboard input
- identifiable user information

As a result, no personal data exists within our systems.

### Legal Basis for Processing (where applicable)

Where limited non-identifiable usage data is processed, the legal bases are:
- **Article 6(1)(b) GDPR** – performance of a contract (core app functionality)
- **Article 6(1)(f) GDPR** – legitimate interest (application stability and improvement)

## 8. Data Transfers

We do not transfer personal data outside of your device.

- No international data transfers occur
- No user data is shared with third parties
